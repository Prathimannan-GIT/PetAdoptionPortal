How Adoption Works

A simple, transparent flow that protects animals and adopters.

Step 1

Browse verified pets

Use filters for location, species, and search. Every profile includes temperament notes and care details.

Step 2

Apply online

Submit your application with required fields and a short message. No empty submissions allowed.

Step 3

Shelter review & meet

Shelters approve or reject based on safety and fit. Meet the pet in-person before final placement.
